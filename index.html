About me

Strategy-led.
Built for growth.

I'm a senior marketing leader with experience across FMCG, not-for-profit and direct-to-consumer brands. I've built and led marketing functions from the ground up, taking real business challenges and turning them into work that connects with people and drives growth.

At the Heart Foundation, I led brand, fundraising and health campaigns at a national level, including a full brand refresh and flagship campaigns that shifted both awareness and behaviour. More recently at Redsbaby, I owned the end-to-end marketing strategy for a scaling DTC business, focused on driving sustainable growth through product launches, brand building, promotions and a 10-year milestone campaign.

What excites me

I'm at my best when I'm working on problems that need to connect insight, creativity and commercial thinking to move something forward.

Right now, I'm particularly interested in how marketing is evolving: from how we think about growth beyond channels, to how new technologies are reshaping discovery, decision-making and measurement. It feels like a moment where a lot of the old ways of working are being challenged, which makes it a really interesting time to be doing this work.

I'm always looking to build on that, whether that's through new tools, better use of data, or simply finding smarter ways to connect brand and performance.
