About me

Strategy-first.
Brand-obsessed.

I'm a senior marketing leader with deep experience across FMCG, not-for-profit, and direct-to-consumer brands. I've built and led marketing functions from the ground up, turning business challenges into campaigns people actually remember.

At the Heart Foundation, I led brand, fundraising and campaign work at national scale — including a full masterbrand refresh and flagship fundraising campaigns. At Redsbaby, I owned end-to-end marketing strategy for a scaling DTC business: product launches, brand awareness, promotions, and a 10-year brand milestone campaign.

I love the work that happens between insight and execution — where strategy meets creativity and something genuinely good gets made.
